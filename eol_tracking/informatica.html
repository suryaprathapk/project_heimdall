    End of Life Dates for Informatica Platform and Services

    Version 5
    Created by Yamini Soundararajan on Mar 7, 2019 12:42 AM. Last modified by Supreetha Rao on May 5, 2021 5:18 AM.

      The Informatica Platform is a set of data services and information solutions powered by a state-of-the-art data management engine.

       

      The end of support for the Informatica Platform includes the following products:

       

      • Enterprise Data Catalog (EDC) and Enterprise Data Preparation (EDP)

      • Data Engineering Integration (DEI) – formerly called Big Data Management (BDM)

      • Data Engineering Streaming (DES) – formerly called Big Data Streaming (BDS)

      • Data Engineering Quality (DEQ) – formerly called Big Data Quality (BDQ)

      • Data Engineering Masking (DEM)

      • Data Quality (DQ)

      • PowerCenter

      • PowerExchange (PWX)

      • Data Transformation (B2B DT)

      • Test Data Management (TDM) and Data Validation Option (DVO) from version 10 onwards

      • Data Integration Hub (DIH) from version 10 onwards

      • B2B Data Exchange (DX) from version 10 onwards

      • Metadata Manager and Business Glossary

       

       

       

      Version Release Date Minimum Support Period End of Support Extended Support Sustaining Support
      10.5 March, 2021 March 31, 2025 TBA TBA TBA
      10.4 December, 2019 March 31, 2024 TBA TBA TBA
      10.2 September, 2017 September 30, 2021 September 30, 2021 September 30,2022 TBA
      10.1.x June, 2016 June 30, 2020 September 30, 2020 September 30, 2021 Not Available

       

       

      From the October 1, 2020, Informatica Platform 10.1 moved to Extended Support until September 30, 2021. Note that Extended Support is available at an additional charge. For further information, please contact: icare@informatica.com

      There will be no further support on Informatica Platform 10.1 after this date. Informatica recommends upgrading to Informatica Platform 10.5. Please contact our Shipping team for the latest 10.5 software download.

       

      For more information on product lifecycle, extended support policy, and end of life details of various Informatica products, view Informatica Product Lifecycle Guide.
